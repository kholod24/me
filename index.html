About Me

Here's a little about me. My name is Ilya, and I've been developing games since I was 8 (I'm now 18). After watching "Indie Game: The Movie", I saw the game creation process in a new light and wanted to make games myself. I first learned to make them in Scratch, then switched to Java. Then the path led me to Unity, and I even managed to participate in a few game jams.

I'm very inspired by Edmund McMillan and Jonathan Blow. And I hope that I can get at least a little closer to their mastery of game creation.
